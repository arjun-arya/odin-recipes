Turkey Picadillo

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Tomato
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chicken Broth
  • Garlic
  • Frozen bell pepper and onion
  • Spices (Bay Leaf, Cumin, Salt, Pepper, Chili Powder
  • Anything you'd like to add (Tomatoes, Lettuce, Mayo, Tahini, etc.)

Steps

  1. Brown Ground Turkey in Hot Pan with vegetable oil
  2. Blend garlic, tomato, onion, cilantro into a "sofrito"
  3. Add spices to turkey
  4. Combine sofrito and turkey in pan once excess moisture has gone away
  5. Add tomato paste, bell pepper and onion, and broth and cook down until favored consistency
