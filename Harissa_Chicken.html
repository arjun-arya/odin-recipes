Harissa Chicken

Ingredients

  • Harissa
  • Chicken Thigh
  • Olive Oil
  • Tortilla
  • Anything you'd like to add (Tomatoes, Lettuce, Mayo, Tahini, etc.)

Steps

  1. Marinate Chicken thighs in Harissa and Olive Oil Mix for at least 20 minutes
  2. Bake Marinated Chicken thighs at 400 degrees F for 18 minutes
  3. Heat tortillas up
  4. Assemble sandwich with chopped chicken and toppings on tortilla
